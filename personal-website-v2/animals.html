Animals

Animals that I like:

Tigers

I like Tigers because they are good hunters in the wild. I also like that they roar loud.

Lions

I like lions because they look strong. As king of the jungle, they are good at hunting and fighting.

Wolves

Wolves are my dream pet. They look so cool and scary at the same time.

Snow Leopards

Snow leopards can keep themselves warm during the winter and hunt during the night.

Saber Tooth Tigers

They are already extinct but I still like them. I think that they will win a fight against lions.

Sharks

Sharks are strong and mighty giants of the ocean. The funny thing is, they are afraid of dolphins.
