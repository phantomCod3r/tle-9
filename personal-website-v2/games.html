My Games

Games that I like:

Minecraft

I like to create buildings and build traps. Minecraft lets me do that even more so with multiplayer mode. With other players, I enjoy joy playing with them, building houses and bases with them, and challenging them to a fight to test out skills. In Minecraft servers, there is a PvP arena where multiple players participate in a giht challenge and the last player standing wins.

Adventure Quest 3D

Adventure Quest 3D or AQ3D is a mobile and pc game. This is an adventure, multiplayer game that has a lot of cool weapons, armors, level ups, and places you can travel or explore with. Also there are a lot of strong bosses that you can fight, not to mention you can form your own guild and lead them, the max member of the guild is up to 20 players. There are no boring moments because there are a lot of missions.

Rules Of Survival

Rules Of Survival or ROS is a online game where can player shot each other and until only one remains. It needs strategy. You can choose solo if you want to kill all players or you can also choose team mates so you can fight other players as a team. The last team standing wins, and by the way when you team up, the player in the team that has the most kills is the MVP.

Beach Buggy Raching 2

Beach Buggy Raching 2 is an online mobile game where you can race with other players but unlike other racing games this racing game has power ups that you can use to shot, knock out, or slowdown the cars of other players. There are a lot of characters you can use and each character has his or her own powers. There are a lot of cars you can buy or unlock. If there is a special events like Chistmas or Halloween, there is a chace you can win a rare car or a rare character.

Rise Of Nation

Rise Of Nation is a strategy war game. First you must build your village from scratch and make it rich. Then, when you have enough resources, you create armies for war and conquer other nations. There are only a few who played this game because it's been so long since has been updated but it still awesome for me. I play this with my mom.

Swordigo

Swordigo is a mobile game where you can have an adventure to kill dark entities. This game has a lot of quests that you must finish. It throws puzzles along the way and yeah, there are a lot of them. Also you must get the magic sword so you can use that to kill the ultimate dark entity and save the kingdom. You get the sword as you defeat bad bosses.
